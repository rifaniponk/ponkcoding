Ayatura is a focused reading companion I designed and built in public over two weeks. Two weeks is not enough time to build everything, which is exactly why it worked.

## Scope is the product

Every feature I cut made the remaining ones sharper. No accounts, no sync, no settings screen — just open the app and read. The constraint forced decisions I would otherwise have deferred for months.

## The launch checklist

I keep a short, boring checklist for every launch: icons at every size, a working share flow, a privacy note, and one real device test per platform. None of it is exciting. All of it is where launches quietly break.

## What shipping taught me

The version I shipped is smaller than the version I imagined, and better for it. The imagined version had every feature. The shipped version had a point of view.
